Preferred, Standard & Challenged


Segmentation of our business into 'Preferred', 'Standard' and 'Challenged' is a key area and will only increase as we identify and improve our sources for this data. The current mappings are either based on Actuarial portfolio reviews or from an Industry code (e.g. NAICS). Anything without an appropriate source is labelled as 'Not Segmented'.

The number of indiviual transactions is limited to 10.000 records, please narrow down your search to less than 10.000 records.
